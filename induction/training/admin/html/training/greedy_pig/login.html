Game Rules

Objective: The objective of Pig is to score 100 points before your opponents.

 

Taking Your Turn: On your turn, follow these steps:

 

a. Roll the die: Roll the die clicking 'Roll Dice'. The number that comes up on the die is your score for this turn.

b. Scoring:

  • If you roll a 1, you lose all the points you've accumulated during your turn, and your turn ends.
  • If you roll a number other than 1, you can choose to either roll again (risking your current turn score) or end your turn and add the current turn score to your total score.

 

Accumulating Points: Keep notice of your total score as you take turns. Remember, you want to reach 100 points to win.

 

Winning the Game: The first player to reach or exceed 100 points wins the game.

 

Strategy: Pig is a game of risk and reward. You'll need to decide when to keep rolling to accumulate more points and when to play it safe and end your turn to avoid rolling a 1 and losing your progress.
